About Me



My name is Sotiris Chatoupis and I'm a passionate self-taught designer with a background in electrical engineering. I believe in combining imagination and logic to create useful products that make people's life better.I am intersted in all stages of design, from the idea to the final product. Invision, Photoshop and Illustrator are my favorite toys. Github, HTML, CSS and JS are some new friends that help me widen my creative horizons.

AROUND THE WEB

Trivitap

Trivitap is the project that turned my passion for design into hard work! It's a real time mobile game. I designed the user interface and user experience of the game.

Herocam

Herocam is an app based on image recognition which aims to make the tourist experience better. I designed the UI nad UX of the app. The aim was to be fresh, easy adn fast to use. It's available on the App Store.

Thanker

Thanker is a prototype I made for a mobile app. The idea is to promote positivity and good deeds through a social network of thanks and wishes.

Book Cover

3d creations

While I was serving my army duty in Greece I practiced 3d design in my free time. My military unit badge and a small church was two things I saw everyday in the army camp and apparently source of inspiration :)

NTUA WANNA KNOW

With more than 10.000 followers, NTUA WANNA KNOW is the most popular social page of the National Technical University of Athens. As a student there and a fan of the page, I was more than happy to design the logo.
